## Ingredients

- 1/4 cup flour
- 2 cups sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1/2 cup buttermilk
- 3 eggs beaten
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 1/4 teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Melt butter in saucepan
2. Add sugar & flour, mix.
3. Add eggs, buttermilk, salt, & vanilla
4. Put in unbaked pie shell
5. Bake at 325* for 45 minutes
