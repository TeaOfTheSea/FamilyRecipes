## Ingredients

- 3-4 bananas crushed
- 1/2 cup butter or crisco
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon sour cream (generous)
- 2 eggs
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 2 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/2 teaspoon baking powder

## Directions

1. Cream butter, sugar, and sour cream
2. Add dry ingredients
3. Mix well
4. Bake at 325* for 1 hour
