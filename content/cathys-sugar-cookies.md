## Ingredients

- 2/3 cup shortening
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 1 1/2 cup flour
- 1 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 4 teaspoons milk

## Directions

1. Cream shortening and sugar
2. Add egg and vanilla
3. Stir in dry ingredients with milk
4. Bake on ungreased cookie sheet for 10-12 minutes at 350*
