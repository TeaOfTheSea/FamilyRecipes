## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 cup all porpose flour
- 1 tablespoon baking powder
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 large egg
- 1 cup milk
- 2 tablespoons crisco oil

## Directions

1. Whisk together all the dry ingredients
2. Add the milk, egg, and oil
3. Stir until moistened
4. Ladel onto a heated griddle
