## Ingredients

- 1 cup chocolate chips
- 1 cup mini marshmallows
- 4 tablespoons milk
- 2 tablespoons rum

## Directions

1. Combine chocolate chips, mini marshmallows, and milk in a glass mixing bowl
2. Microwave for one minute
3. Stir mixture
4. Microwave for one minute
5. Add rum
